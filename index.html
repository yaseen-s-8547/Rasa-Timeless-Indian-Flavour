Our Legacy

In a small mud house, where the firewood crackled and spices danced in the air, Rasa was born. Passed down from Nani to Amma, every dish holds a story, every flavour carries memory. Welcome to timeless Indian taste.

Menu Through Time

1600s-Hyderabadi Biriyani

Rich rice dish layered with spices and slow-cooked mutton, introduced by Mughal Nawabs

1800s – Idli & Dosa

Fermented batter of rice and urad dal became the staple of Tamil kitchens and temple kitchens.

1947 – Samosa & Chai

Snack of revolutionaries — chai tapris became mini-courts of free speech post-Independence.
